I designed my character to be simple so it would be easier to animate. I set up bones for the body, head, arms, and legs, but at first it was really confusing figuring out how to attach the sprites and paint the mesh correctly. Sometimes the sprites even disappeared when I tried to key a frame, which was frustrating. I fixed it by double-checking which sprites were assigned to which bones and carefully painting the weights. For the animation, I mostly keyed the rotations of the limbs and let Godot fill in the movement between frames. Overall, it was tricky at first, and I know I could have planned my bones and keyframes better to make things go faster.
